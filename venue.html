V e n u e



Swancar Farm Country House is located in Trowell, where Emily grew up. From the venue, you are able to see the location of our first date (Wollaton Hall), one of Norbit's favourite galloping spots (Pit Lane), and the remains of Emily's secondary school (Bramcote Hills Sport and Community College) The entire day will be held here so no need to drive from place to place!

We hope you love the venue as much as we do and we can't wait to see you there on the 28th November 2020!

L o c a t i o n

By Car

It is just a short drive from the M1 J25 (15 mins) with plenty of on-site parking if you do wish to drive.

By Train

The closest train stations are Ilkeston and Nottingham with taxis available from the stations and locally.

By Air

Only a 30 minute drive from East Midlands Airport.
